About The Game

Survive Min is a psychological horror visual novel centered around a single terrifying night. Strange noises echo outside your home. People around the neighborhood have started disappearing. Bodies are being found nearby.

Then one night, the sounds stop coming from outside. They begin coming from inside your room.

The game revolves around Min — an unpredictable entity whose behavior constantly shifts between affectionate, manipulative, playful, unsettling, and dangerous.

Why Players Love It

Psychological Tension
