# Survive Min Online

## About Survive Min

Survive Min is a psychological horror visual novel centered around a single terrifying night inside your home.

The story begins with strange noises outside your house. People around the neighborhood have started disappearing, and bodies are being discovered nearby.

Then one night, the sounds stop coming from outside.

They begin coming from inside your room.

The game revolves around Min — an unpredictable entity trapped inside with the protagonist. Throughout the story, Min constantly shifts between affectionate, manipulative, playful, unsettling, and dangerous behavior, creating an emotionally unstable atmosphere where even simple dialogue choices can drastically change the outcome.

The experience combines:
- psychological horror
- yandere themes
- branching narrative paths
- emotional tension
- multiple endings
- survival-based dialogue choices
